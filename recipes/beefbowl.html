Beef Bowl Recipe

Description of Recipe: The beef bowl recipe consists of just jasmine white rice and extrea lean ground beef seasoned with montreal steak spice and topped off with a teryiaki sauce.

Recipe Ingredients

  • 1-2 cups of extra lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup of Jasmine white rice
  • 3/4 a cup of water for the 1/2 cup of jasmine rice
  • 3 tbps of teriyaki sauce (homemode or store bought)

Receipe Steps

  1. Bring to a boil 1/2 cup of jasmine white rice with 3/4 a cup of water
  2. Simmer rice for 15 mins
  3. While rice is cooking, grab 1-2 cups of extra lean ground beef onto a pan
  4. Bring burner to medium high and cook beef until fully brown
  5. Apply montreal steak spice to cooked ground beef and turn up heat to high
  6. Couple minutes on high to crisp beef with spice
  7. Put beef to low and apply teriyaki sauce
  8. Once rice is done, fluff with fork and let it rest for 5 minutes
  9. Once beef and rice are complete, put together and wallah!
