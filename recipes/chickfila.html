Lazy Doordash Chick-fil-a

If you are too lazy to cook, this is the recipe for you!

Recipe Ingredients

  • Phone
  • Doordash preferrably with dash pass
  • Roughly 20 - 30$ CAD
  • Address for delivery

Recipe Steps

  1. Grab Phone
  2. Open up Doordash app
  3. Select Chick-fil-a
  4. Select desired meal
  5. Order to checkout
  6. Await Delivery!
